# Excel-Sales-Analytics

## Sales Report :

- **Project objective:** 
 
 **1.** Created a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_

  To identify growth patterns and compare sales percentages between consecutive years.
  To highlight top-performing customers and detect significant changes in revenue growth.

  **Purpose of Sales Analytics:**

  To understand how each customer contributes to total revenue and overall business performance.
  To track sales trends over time, enabling better forecasting and decision-making.

  **Importance of Analyzing Sales Data:**

  Helps identify high-growth customers and markets, enabling focused resource allocation.
  Provides insights into overall sales performance, highlighting areas needing attention or optimization.

  **2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and Sales Target](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target.pdf)_

  **Purpose of sales analytics:**
 
  To understand how each market contributes to total business growth and identify patterns in sales performance across regions.
  To provide insights that help organizations optimize forecasts, improve planning, and focus on markets with strategic potential.

  **Importance of analyzing sales data:**

  Helps identify high-growth markets and highlight markets performing below target, enabling corrective actions.
  Supports data-driven decision-making by revealing performance gaps

  **3.** Identify the top selling products based on Net Sales _[Top 10 products based on Net Sales](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Top%2010%20Products%20based%20on%20Net%20Sales.pdf)_

 **Objective:**

  To compare product-wise sales performance between 2020 and 2021 and determine which products achieved the highest sales and growth percentage.

 **Report Highlights:**

  Total Net Sales increased from $6.4M in 2020 to $52.0M in 2021, an impressive 808% overall growth.
  AQ Mx NB saw the highest growth rate at 5723.5%, followed by AQ Smash 2 and AQ LION series products.
  Consistent performance improvements across all product categories reflect strong brand expansion and increased market demand.

 **4.** Identify the Net Sales for 2020 and 2021 by division _[Division Level Report](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Division%20Level%20Report.pdf)_

 **Objective:**

  To evaluate sales growth and performance across different divisions between 2020 and 2021, identifying the strongest revenue-generating segments.

 **Report Highlights:**

  Total sales grew from $196.7M in 2020 to $598.9M in 2021, marking an overall 304.5% growth.
  The PC Division recorded the highest growth rate of 413.7%, followed by P&A (321.5%) and N&S (184.4%).
  Consistent performance improvement across divisions indicates effective business expansion and product diversification.

   **5.** Identify products that ranked the top and bottom in terms of Quantity _[Top and Bottom 5 products based by QTY](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Top%20and%20Bottom%20products%20by%20Qty.pdf)_

 **Objective:**

  To analyze the sales volume distribution among products, identifying which products contributed most and least to total quantities sold.

 **Report Highlights:**

  Top 5 Products collectively sold around 19.0 million units, led by AQ Master Wired x1 Ms and AQ Master Wireless x1 Ms.
  Bottom 5 Products recorded a total of 174,887 units, with AQ Home Allin1 Gen 2 having the lowest quantity.
  The significant gap between top and bottom performers highlights opportunities for product optimization and marketing focus.

  **6.** New products in 2021 _[New Products that AtliQ began selling in 2021](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/New%20Products%20-%202021%20Report.pdf)_

 **Objective:**

  To analyze the sales distribution and performance of all new products introduced in 2021 and understand their contribution to total revenue.

 **Report Highlights:**

  A total of $176.2 million in sales was generated by new products in 2021.
  The diversity of product performance shows the success of new market entries across multiple categories.

  **7.** Identify Net sales of Top 5 countries  _[Top 5 countries in terms of Net Sales 2021](https://github.com/Vidhyabharathi08/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Top%205%20Countries%20by%20Net%20Sales%202021.pdf)_

 **Objective:**

  To identify the top-performing countries by total net sales in 2021 and provide a concise business insight into regional sales distribution.

 **Report Highlights:**

  India recorded the highest net sales at $161.3M, contributing significantly to overall revenue.
  USA followed with $87.8M, while South Korea, Canada, and United Kingdom made up the remaining top contributors.
